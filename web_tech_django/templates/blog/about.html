About tour-de-Qazaqstan

Tour-de-Qazaqstan is a tour operator in Almaty which organizes and conducts individual tours and excursions in Kazakhstan and Central Asia since 2015.

We help to travel safely and comfortably, away from noisy and large groups of tourists, because only in this way you can feel the beauty of the world and stay alone with nature.

The main mode of transport in our company is full-drive off-road vehicles. Jeeps will take our customers to the most remote places in Kazakhstan, Kyrgyzstan and Uzbekistan. Time saving, comfort and clear pictures while driving are only a part of what this mode of transport can boast. When travelling long distances, rest on the road is very important.

  • Are you planning to visit Kazakhstan or Central Asia?
  • Have you already bought tickets to Almaty or Nur-Sultan and are looking for a tour?
  • Are you already in Almaty or Nur-Sultan and want to go on a trip the next day?
  • A tour bought with another company cancelled and you want to start on a trip as soon as possible?

In all these cases, please contact us and we will definitely help you. Our consultants answer within 2 hours and are available 24 hours 7 days a week.

Reasons to travel with
tour-de-Qazaqstan
  • We provide a car with a guide 30 minutes before departure
  • We carry out technical inspection of vehicles after each tour
  • We know how to glue a tire in the middle of the steppe
  • We will show you how to avoid a tick bite and treat the wound if you get hurt
  • We will find an individual approach to any customer
